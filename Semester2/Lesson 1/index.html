Background

Ryan Flaharty is a student at Torrey Pines High School who enjoys watching west bay area sports, including the Warriors, and playing rugby.

Rugby

Ryan has been playing rugby for four years now. He has been heavily involved in the growth of rugby at TPHS, by helping the team with fund raisers and constantly trying to recruit footballers to come out and play.

Bay Area pride

Born in the bay area, Ryan is an avid 49ers, Giants, and Warriors fan. He tries to go to as many Giants games as possible in the summer
